About

Goal

Our goal is to give people a way to share files with each other quickly and effortlessly. Think of Dropbucket as the clipboard for files that you don't have to worry about managing.

How to Use

Dropbucket is designed for ease of use. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Click Upload to upload a file.
  2. Click a file's button to copy its link to your system clipboard.
  3. Share the link! Anyone with the link can download the file until it expires.
All files are permanently deleted after they expire. Dropbucket is NOT intended to provide long-term storage.
